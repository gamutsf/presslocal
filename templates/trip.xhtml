Winery Hopping in Healdsburg

Where to sip on the way to, and in the heart of, downtown Healdsburg

  • Transportation
  • Directions
  • Share
Add All Save Trip
  • Distance 13 Miles
  • Total Time Hrs

Overview

On the way to Healdsburg you may become overwhelmed with the wealth of wineries lined up along the 101 -- not to mention the abundance of tasting rooms that take up the majority real estate in the small city's downtown. Unfortunately time and tastebuds won't allow you to stop everywhere, so we've lined up just a few of our top stops in the area. This 30 mile trip will take you from the edge of Sebastapool, through the scenic Westside Road, and right to the epicenter of downtown Healdsburg. Once you're there and done with your wine tasting, don't forget to grab a bit to eat at one of Michael Bauer's recommended restaurants as well. -January 2017

Rochioli Vineyards and Winery

6192 Westside Rd, Healdsburg, CA 95448, United States
Add to Trip

Before you step through the doors of the Rochioli tasting room, walk past them onto the patio and take a look at the sprawling vineyards. This is the same location the Rochioli family has farmed since the 1930s. Granted, at the time, the main crops were hops, string beans and prunes, with few actual vineyard plantings. But the family found their vine-planting niche, and the property now includes 162 acres dedicated to Sauvignon Blanc, Chardonnay and Pinot Noir. So when you look out into that field, you're looking at a bit of family and wine country history. And when you step inside the winery doors, you'll be able to taste it. Read the full review.
