January 12, 2017, 5:00 pm

Great Grape Study - Sangiovese

5:00 pm

Back Room Wines

1000 Main St, Napa, CA

Add to Trip

Details

Our "Great Grape Study" series is BACK for 2017.

These Thursday evening tastings study the same grape made from different parts of the world and in different styles.

This is a very good way to gain greater appreciation for a grape.

The Great Grape Study on Thursday, January 12 turns to the greatest grape of Tuscany: SANGIOVESE.

We narrow the geographical scope as Sangiovese is so good in one place: Tuscany. We have for you 4 Tuscan wines, including 3 Brunello di Montalcino from a producer we just met as we tasted (and loved) his wines. See below.

$15/tasting.

Wines poured on sale for 10% off during the tasting.

It's this Thursday, January 12 from 5 to 8 O'Clock.

Walk-in tasting. Come in anytime from 5 to 8 and enjoy.

Reservation not necessary.

Here's the Great SANGIOVESE line-up:

PODERE IL PALAZZINO Chianti Classico

"Grosso Sanese" 2010 $36

TERRALSOLE Brunello di Montalcino 2010 $50

TERRALSOLE Brunello di Montalcino

"Vigna Pian Bossolino" 2010 $55

TERRALSOLE Brunello di Montalcino "Riserva" 2006 $75

VILLA RAGAZZI Sangiovese Napa Valley 2013 $38

Thank you. See you at Back Room!

8:00pm - 10:00pm
